DOG SPACE POSTER

This poster was made for my dog Kubo, I took a side profile picture of his face and imported it into photoshop, I cropped out the rest of the body and created a mask fo the face. I then added a space with filtered effects on the mask and added some bubble effects around the mask to give it the illusion of float. The final touch was to add the japanese text in the top left corner.
